Hello. My name is Manuel Geraldes.

I'm a game developer currently based in Lisbon, Portugal.

About Me

During my bachelor's degree in Videogames, I've worked on multiple projects, both individual and as part of a team. In this time, I've developed basic and advanced skills in various tools for the production of multimedia applications.

As part of my degree, I interned at a secondary school, where I assisted students during their videogame projects in a Computer Applications class, by creating simple prototypes and giving them tips on how to better implement their ideas. Besides that, I developed a game to be featured in the school's webpage, based around the history of the institution's patron.

I am always looking for opportunities to improve as a developer.

My Projects

Mole

An espionage mission with focus on puzzle solving


A first-person puzzle game, where the player controls an undercover Russian spy in an American institution, on a mission to retrieve a classified document that will greatly benefit their nation.

I did most of the level and puzzle design, having also programmed the access level mechanic, and been involved in the development of most gameplay features.

Virtual Friend

Select and take care of your companion


A Tamagotchi inspired virtual pet game, where the player has to take care of a creature of their choice, by answering all their needs.

This project was done in collaboration with CICANT, created as an accessible and stimulating audiovisual experience, aimed at individuals with physical and intellectual limitations.

I worked as a programmer, developing most gameplay aspects, and adjusting values in order to make the game playable for the target audience.

Detective Tales

A noir-themed story experienced as an interactive comic book


A single-player 3D interactive fiction with action/adventure elements, played in the pages of a comic book where each panel offers a new piece of gameplay.

I was responsible for the development of the first chapter, which involved the creation of the environments and mechanics associated with this part of the game. I also did the framing of each panel, assuring that the camera and all visible objects were in a good position, and assembled the soundscapes heard throughout the game.

Diner

Simple restaurant waiter time wasting experience


A Point & Click time management game, where the goal is to serve as many customers as possible within a small period.

Initially made as a simple prototype to aid the students in a Computer Applications class, this project was further developed in Unity.

A Quiet Life

Rewind time to get through your daily routine


A single-player Point & Click adventure in a 2D first-person perspective, where time manipulation plays and important role.

I was one of the game designers, having defined the main mechanics and systems for the game, while also working on the narrative and backstory. I was involved in the overall level design and puzzle creation, being responsible for assembling the soundscapes that are integrated into the puzzles. I was the sole programmer for this project.

Stuart

Two characters navigating through their creator's history


A single-player 2D side-scroller following two established comic book characters in a new adventure, going through key moments in their own creator's life, unbeknownst to them.

A solo project, developed for a secondary school's website, based on its patron's comic book series and history.
